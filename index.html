Community Spotlights

A closer look at a few organizations making a difference in Oxford. All highlighted resources can be found in our main directory.

Oxford Food Pantry

Food & Basic Needs Free

The Oxford Food Pantry provides emergency groceries and staple items for individuals and families in Lafayette County, ensuring that no one in our community has to go without a meal.

  • Location: Oxford, MS
  • Serves: Individuals & families
  • How to access: Walk-in during pantry hours; ID requested but not required.

Oxford–Lafayette Literacy Council

Education Tutoring

Volunteer tutors offer free literacy support, ESL classes, and academic coaching for youth and adults who want to strengthen their reading and writing skills.

  • Serves: Youth & adults
  • Focus: Reading, writing, English language learning

Mental Health Referrals
