About This Project

The Oxford Community Resource Hub was created by the Oxford High School TSA Webmaster team for the 2026 TSA competition. Our goal is to make it easier for residents to find local support services, non-profit organizations, and community programs in one place.

While we are students, we designed this site with community members in mind: clear navigation, simple language, and mobile-friendly pages that can be accessed on phones, tablets, and computers.

How We Built It

  • Plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript (no external frameworks).
  • An interactive directory that filters resources by category, cost, and who they serve.
  • A public suggestion form so neighbors can recommend new resources.
  • Accessibility-conscious design with high contrast, clear headings, and keyboard-friendly navigation.
